Next time you visit Amsterdam, make sure you head to Amsterdam's latest attraction - The A'dam lookout and whilst you're there, go on the 'Over the edge' swing - for 5 euros you will not be diappointed. Its definitely for the adrenaline junkies in your life and i loved it!
